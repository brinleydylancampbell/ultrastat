ULTRASTAT Website Development Brief  
Objective  
The website is not an ecommerce website.  
ULTRASTAT is sold primarily through distribution and product trial/demo, therefore the website’s role is to:  
1\.	Generate demo requests   
2\.	Support distributor sales   
3\.	Generate international distributor/importer enquiries   
4\.	Improve conversion from Meta/Facebook ads   
5\.	Increase credibility and trust internationally   
The website should function as a sales and lead-generation machine, not just an information brochure.  
\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_  
Business Model Context  
ULTRASTAT is generally sold through:  
Marketing / awareness  
↓  
Demo request  
↓  
Local distributor trial  
↓  
User experiences product  
↓  
Sale  
The website should support this process.

Priority 1: Conversion Improvements (Highest Priority)  
Homepage improvements  
The homepage should be more conversion-focused and outcome-driven.  
Focus on:  
•	Reducing dust contamination   
•	Better metallic laydown/orientation   
•	Improved finish quality   
•	Less polishing/rework   
•	Ease of use   
Less technical jargon.  
More painter/bodyshop language.  
Strong calls-to-action throughout.

Demo Request System  
Create a stronger and more visible:  
“Request a Demo”  
The goal is to route enquiries to distributors.
